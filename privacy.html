THE PROTOCOL.

LAST UPDATED: DECEMBER 2025

01. PRIVACY POLICY

Your identity is your business. MK operates on a "Zero-Data" philosophy.

  • Data Collection: We do not store your phone number or real name after a session is completed.
  • No Content Policy: MK will never record audio, take photos, or post about a client on social media without explicit written consent.
  • Confidentiality: Any conversations held during "Doing Nothing" sessions are strictly confidential and will never be shared with third parties.

02. TERMS OF SERVICE

By booking MK, you agree to the following social contract:

"MK is a professional companion, not a laborer, friend-for-hire, or romantic interest."
  • Platonic Boundary: Zero physical contact. Any violation results in immediate termination of the session with no refund.
  • Safety: Meetups are restricted to public venues (malls, cafes, parks, theaters). Private residences or secluded areas are strictly prohibited.
  • Illegal Acts: MK will not participate in, or be a witness to, any illegal activities.

03. FINANCIALS

  • Booking Fee: Must be paid via UPI to secure the slot. This covers the "presence" fee.
  • Expenses: All travel (Uber/Auto/Metro), food, and entry tickets for MK must be covered by the client at the time of the session.
  • Cancellations: Cancellations made less than 24 hours before the slot are non-refundable.
